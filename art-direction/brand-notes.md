# Quid brand notes

## Personality

Quid is calm, capable, attentive, warm, organized, and hospitality-led. The visual and verbal tone is premium but approachable. Quid is charming without becoming childish, loud, robotic, or hyper-cartoonish.

## Palette

- Deep green: `#1E3D34`
- Muted green: `#47685B`
- Soft sage: `#A9B5A6`
- Warm sand: `#E7DAC4`
- Cream: `#FBF7EF`
- Terracotta: `#C0714B`

Use deep green for primary text and controls, cream and warm white for reading surfaces, sand and sage for section changes, and terracotta as a restrained accent. Maintain WCAG 2.2 AA contrast.

## Typography

Manrope is the functional sans serif for body copy, navigation, buttons, forms, labels, and product interfaces. Newsreader is an editorial accent used sparingly for short emotional phrases and pull quotes. Both are bundled locally as variable WOFF2 fonts with swap behavior.

## Spacing and shapes

Use generous section spacing, 24–30px large-card radii, 16–20px small-card radii, 14–16px button radii, thin warm borders, and broad low-opacity shadows. Compositions can be asymmetric but must remain balanced and easy to scan.

## Mascot rules

The approved Quid mascot is canonical. Preserve the tall rounded head, calm dark-green eyes, gentle smile, cream body, green concierge vest, terracotta scarf, Q badge, simplified tentacles, and soft shading. Do not redraw, recolor, change proportions, or generate a different mascot. Use the named approved poses for context and keep the face visible at all sizes.

## Logo use

Use the approved full-color wordmark on light surfaces. One-color assets may be used on deep-green or monochrome surfaces. Do not use the discontinued “Guiding families home” lockup as primary messaging; the site tagline is “More tours. Less chasing.”

## Image treatment

Mascot sources live in `src/assets/brand/` and are processed by Astro into responsive WebP derivatives. Product interfaces, dashboards, calculators, messages, and diagrams remain real HTML and CSS rather than screenshots.

## Animation

Motion is subtle: 3–6px ambient movement, gentle reveal transitions, and restrained panel changes. Avoid bouncing, rapid waving, large rotations, and attention-seeking loops. Every experience remains complete with reduced motion enabled.

## Accessibility

Maintain semantic landmarks, logical headings, visible focus, keyboard navigation, 44px touch targets, labelled fields, reduced motion, non-color-only status cues, meaningful alt text, and high-contrast reading surfaces.
