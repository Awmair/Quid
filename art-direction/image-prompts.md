# Quid image-production prompts

These prompts document future art direction. Any new production must use the approved Quid mascot pack as a strict image reference and must be reviewed against the canonical brand board before use.

## Shared identity lock

Preserve exactly the approved Quid mascot: tall rounded squid head, large calm dark-green eyes, gentle smile, warm cream body, deep-green concierge vest, terracotta scarf, small Q badge, graceful simplified tentacles, soft premium shading, calm hospitality personality, transparent background, no text, no watermark, no redesign, no proportion changes.

## Hero mascot

Using the shared identity lock, show Quid standing in a calm three-quarter pose with a small admissions clipboard and a deep-green welcome card. Friendly, composed posture; premium editorial character lighting; transparent background; full body and tentacles visible.

## Inquiry pose

Using the shared identity lock, show Quid calmly acknowledging a new family website inquiry with one open welcoming tentacle and a small non-text notification card. Transparent background; no embedded copy or interface screenshot.

## Tour pose

Using the shared identity lock, show Quid presenting an elegant tour-calendar tablet, angled toward the viewer. Keep the tablet screen abstract and text-free so the real calendar can be built in HTML.

## Human-handoff pose

Using the shared identity lock, show Quid calmly passing a simple message card to a warm professional admissions staff member in non-clinical business attire. No doctors, hospital room, medical chart, medication, exam, diagnosis, or clinical symbol.

## Daily-summary pose

Using the shared identity lock, show Quid holding a concise morning admissions summary card with abstract lines only. Calm, organized expression; transparent background.

## Final-CTA pose

Using the shared identity lock, show Quid giving one small, calm wave. Avoid energetic celebration, bouncing, exaggerated motion, or multiple waving tentacles.

## Favicon icon

Create a simplified head-and-scarf Quid emblem recognizable at 16, 32, and 48 pixels. Use only deep green, cream, and restrained terracotta. Strong silhouette, minimal internal detail, transparent background.

## One-color mark

Create a clean monochrome Quid head and simplified tentacle emblem that works in deep green on cream and cream on deep green. Preserve the approved silhouette and facial proportions. Proof manually before large-format or trademark use.

## Open Graph artwork

1200×630 landscape social card. Approved Quid mascot at right, cream and warm-sand ground, deep-green title area, terracotta accent, and an abstract admissions-dashboard motif. Include only the approved text “Quid” and “More tours. Less chasing.” in large legible typography. No unapproved claims.

## 404 illustration

Using the shared identity lock, show Quid calmly studying an upside-down map or misplaced inquiry card. Thoughtful rather than worried; transparent background; no embedded text.
