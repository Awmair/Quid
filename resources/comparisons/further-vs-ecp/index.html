Where might each option fit better?

Further may be a stronger fit when

The operator keeps a CRM and wants multi-channel AI engagement and tour conversion tools.

ECP CRM may be a stronger fit when

The operator needs a purpose-built CRM and values ECP clinical and operational connections.

Which option is more appropriate for a single assisted living community?

A single community retaining its CRM may consider Further for channel expansion; one replacing its CRM or aligning with ECP operations may consider ECP. Confirm implementation scope before comparing cost.

Which option is more appropriate for a multi-community operator?

ECP publicly describes portfolio reporting and a broader operational platform. Further may fit a portfolio standardizing digital engagement, but portfolio controls should be demonstrated.
