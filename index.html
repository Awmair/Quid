Quid knows when to stop

Automation for the routine parts. Real people for the important parts.

When a family shares medical information, asks about care eligibility, discusses medication, or needs a sensitive conversation, Quid pauses and alerts the designated staff member.

Quid does not

  • Recommend a level of care
  • Interpret medical information
  • Assess clinical eligibility
  • Discuss medications
  • Make clinical promises
  • Impersonate an employee

Quid does

  • Acknowledge the family
  • Flag the conversation
  • Notify the right person
  • Keep the request visible
  • Record the handoff

Questions senior living teams ask about Quid

What is Quid?

Quid handles new inquiries, family follow-up, and tour scheduling behind the scenes for senior living communities. It helps teams miss fewer inquiries and book more tours without adding another admin hire.

Is Quid a CRM?

No. Quid is not a CRM replacement. It works around a focused admissions workflow and can connect with the inquiry form, calendar, email process, and lead system your team already uses.

Does Quid replace our admissions team?

No. Quid handles repetitive communication and coordination so staff have more time for residents, tours, family conversations, and decisions that need human judgment.

Does Quid answer incoming calls?

The initial version focuses on website inquiries, email communication, tour scheduling, and staff handoffs. Voice and SMS may be offered separately later.

Can Quid answer clinical questions?

No. Medical, clinical, medication, eligibility, and care-assessment questions are routed to a designated staff member.

What does Quid handle first?

Quid starts narrowly with one community, one website inquiry source, and one tour calendar. The first workflow usually covers acknowledgement, tour coordination, approved follow-up, visibility, and staff handoffs.

Who is Quid built for?

Quid is built for assisted living, memory care, independent living, and skilled nursing admissions teams that want fewer missed inquiries and more booked tours without adding another daily tool or admin hire.
