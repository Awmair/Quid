Human-review rule

When the workflow is unsure, it stops.

The designated staff member receives the conversation, the question and the next action—without asking the family to repeat everything.

04 / 04Your community
30-day action plan
Start with one source, one calendar and one accountable owner.
The quickest useful implementation is deliberately narrow. Prove the handoff works before adding more channels or complexity.
Week 1
Map and approve
Document the current form, recipient inbox, response copy, escalation rules and tour-calendar owner.
Week 2
Connect and test
Connect one inquiry source and test normal, unanswered and human-review scenarios.
Week 3
Run with guardrails
Launch the approved workflow, review daily summaries and correct exceptions quickly.
Week 4
Measure and decide
Review response coverage, speed, tours, handoffs and what should—or should not—expand.
Keep the scorecard honest
Measure movement, not AI activity.
