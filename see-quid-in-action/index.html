Dashboard questions

Understand the admissions dashboard at a glance.

Is the dashboard real?

The page shows the Quid product interface and its admissions workflow states. The records explain how the interface works and are not customer performance claims.

What does the sample dashboard show?

It shows new inquiries, tour offers, booked tours, follow-up activity, daily totals, next actions, and items that require staff attention.

Can this connect to our current tools?

Quid is designed to work around one existing inquiry source, tour calendar, and admissions workflow first. Connection details depend on the tools involved.

Does Quid make admissions decisions?

No. Quid coordinates routine non-clinical follow-up. Admissions, eligibility, clinical, and care decisions remain with staff.

What does “Needs Attention” mean?

It means the workflow has paused because a reply, sensitive topic, overdue action, or exception needs a staff member to review it.
