Starter policy

Privacy

This page explains the information this marketing website may collect and how it may be used. It requires legal review before public launch.

Last updated: July 10, 2026

About this website

get-quid.site is a marketing website for Quid, an AI-assisted admissions follow-up service for senior living communities. Quid is not a senior living facility and does not provide residential, clinical, medical, or emergency care.

Information you may submit

Meeting and pilot forms may request business contact and operational information, such as your name, work email, job title, community name, website, number of locations, scheduling or lead-management tools, and a description of the admissions follow-up problem you want to discuss.

Do not submit resident names, diagnoses, medication information, medical records, protected health information, Social Security numbers, payment-card information, or other sensitive personal information through this website.

Why information is collected

Information submitted through a configured form or booking link may be used to respond to your request, arrange a meeting, assess pilot fit, communicate about Quid, and improve the marketing and sales process.

External form and booking providers

This static website cannot process forms by itself. When configured, it may send form information to an external form provider or link to an external booking provider. Those providers process information under their own terms and privacy practices. The selected providers should be documented here before launch.

Analytics

The website may optionally use lightweight, privacy-conscious analytics when configured by the site owner. It does not include advertising pixels, heatmaps, or session replay by default.

Data sharing and retention

Business contact information should be shared only with providers needed to operate the website and respond to inquiries, or when required by law. Retention periods and provider details must be finalized as part of legal review.

Your choices

You may ask the Quid team to update or delete marketing contact information, subject to applicable obligations. A privacy contact email has not yet been configured.

Changes

This starter policy may be updated as the website, providers, and services change. The final policy should identify the legal company responsible for the website and the effective contact method.
